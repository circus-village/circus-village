An enchanted private island near the Kouang Si waterfall in Laos, we host esoteric events by reknowned teachers in an untouched paradise.

The village is nested between two rivers with countless natural pools, private beaches and the precious shade of a gigantic bamboo garden surrounded by a tropical teak forest and hill tribe villages.
