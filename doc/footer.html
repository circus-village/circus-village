## Getting There
[![satellite map](assets/img/satellite.jpg)](https://www.google.com/maps/@19.7577325,101.9932579,236m/data=!3m1!1e3u)The village is located 15 minutes walk from the extraordinary Kouang Si waterfall national park, 29 kilometres from Luang Prabang, the historical capital of Laos. [![meeting point](assets/img/meeting-point.jpg)](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Joma+Bakery+Caf%C3%A9/@19.8885404,102.1305129,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m2!3m1!1s0x312f2a426fb70085:0xb1ae7742ff2c3baf)You can fly from Bangkok to the new international airport at Luang Prabang with [Air Asia](http://www.airasia.com/), on the day of the event the circus trucks will collect you from the meeting point at Joma bakery next to the post office in Luang Prabang.
